What is Digital Access?

Digital access is the ability to fully participate in digital society. This includes access to tools and technologies, such as the Internet and computers, that allow for full participation. Unfortunately, not everyone has complete digital access and therefore, are not able to fully participate in digital society.



Advantages of Digital Access :
1. increased access to education.
2. higher productivity levels.
3. more efficient communication.
4. ease of networking.
5. faster dissemination of news.
6. ease of information storage.
7. expansion in job opportunities.

Disadvantages of Digital Access :
1. Data Security.
2. Crime and Terrorism.
3. Complexity.
4. Privacy Concerns.
5. Social Disconnect.
6. Work Overload.
7. Digital Media Manipulation.
